When Maya and her little brother Leo build a scarecrow to chase away the crows haunting their new farmhouse, they think they’ve solved their problem. But after they give it a face, a grin, and a name, Patches starts appearing in places it shouldn’t.

Now the scarecrow is getting closer to the house, and Maya must uncover the secret of the field before Patches decides her family belongs to it forever.

Pretend friends should not need a bedroom.

When Dylan’s family moves into a creepy old house, his little brother Teddy quickly makes a new imaginary friend named Jonah. At first, Dylan thinks it’s just another game.

The Thing in Locker 325

Some lockers should stay closed.

Owen Mercer just wants to survive sixth grade without being noticed. But when he and his best friend Milo are assigned Locker 325, they discover a tiny black hole hiding inside.

At first, the hole seems helpful. Homework disappears. Quizzes vanish. Problems go away. Then the locker starts asking for MORE.

After one terrible mistake, a bully named Chase Dunleavy begins disappearing from school, from people’s memories, and maybe from the world itself. Now Owen has to fix what they did before Locker 325 takes someone else.
